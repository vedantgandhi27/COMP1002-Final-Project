Our story began in 1964, when Bill Bowerman and Phil Knight, two visionary minds, came together with a simple mission: to provide athletes with the tools they need to achieve their goals. What started as a small operation in Oregon, USA, has since grown into a global brand that transcends borders, cultures, and limitations.

HISTORY OF NIKE AIR MAX

Nike revolutionized the footwear industry with the introduction of its groundbreaking cushioning technology known as Air Max. It all started with NASA aeronautical engineer Frank Rudy, who first captured high-density gasses in a urethane air bag, and later brought the idea to Nike as a footwear cushioning concept.
